Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 June 2026

Dayzi ("Dayzi", "we", "us", "our") is a social events application that helps people discover, create, and join local events. Dayzi is owned and operated by Solika Ventures Pvt Ltd, a company incorporated in India.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal data. It applies to:

  • the Dayzi mobile application (the "App"); and
  • the Dayzi website at dayzi.live (the "Site"), including our waitlist / early-registration sign-up.

By creating an account, joining our waitlist, or using the App or Site, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use Dayzi.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: first name, last name, email address, password, age, gender, username
  • Profile information: profile photo, bio ("about me"), favourite song, favourite show, user vibes, profile prompts
  • Event information: event title, description, category, location, date/time, cover image
  • Communications: messages sent in event group chats, event join requests
  • Preferences: interests, privacy settings, notification preferences, theme preference
  • Face / identity verification: if you complete face verification, we process a selfie image (with a gesture for liveness) to confirm you are a real person (see Section 3)
  • Waitlist (Site): the email address you submit on dayzi.live to join our early-registration list

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: device type, operating system version, app version
  • Location data: city and country (derived from device location services, used to show nearby events). We do not collect or store precise GPS coordinates continuously.
  • Usage data: events viewed, joined, and created; app interactions; session duration
  • Push notification tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device tokens for delivering push notifications
  • Crash and performance data: app crash logs and performance metrics (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Analytics data: anonymous app usage patterns (via Firebase Analytics). The dayzi.live website does not currently use analytics.

1.3 Information From Third Parties

  • If you sign in using a third-party service (e.g., Google or Apple), we receive basic profile information as authorised by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data to:

  • Provide the service: create your account, display your profile, enable event creation and discovery, and facilitate group chats and event coordination
  • Personalise your experience: show relevant local events based on your city/country and interests
  • Communicate with you: send push notifications about events you joined, event invitations, request updates, admin announcements, and waitlist updates for the Site
  • Keep Dayzi safe: detect and prevent abuse, spam, fake events, and inappropriate content using automated content moderation and face verification
  • Improve Dayzi: understand how the App is used to improve features and fix bugs
  • Comply with the law: respond to legal requests and enforce our Terms

Legal basis (GDPR): we process your data based on (a) your consent (provided at signup / waitlist sign-up), (b) performance of our contract with you (our Terms), and (c) our legitimate interests in providing a safe, functional service.

3. Content Moderation and AI Processing

To keep Dayzi safe, we use automated systems:

  • Image moderation: uploaded images (profile photos, event cover images) are analysed by SightEngine, a third-party content-moderation service, to detect inappropriate content (e.g., nudity, violence). Images flagged as inappropriate are automatically removed.
  • Text moderation: event names, descriptions, and other text are analysed by Google Gemini AI to detect offensive, hateful, or inappropriate language.
  • AI-generated descriptions: we offer an optional feature that uses Google Gemini AI to generate event descriptions from event details you provide.
  • Face verification: we use Google Gemini AI to compare a selfie you provide (together with a liveness gesture) against your profile photo, to verify that you are a real, unique person. The selfie is used only for this check: it is sent to Google Gemini for the comparison and is not stored by Dayzi. We retain only a verification status (whether you passed) and the date. Images sent to Google Gemini are processed under Google's paid API terms, under which Google does not use them to train or improve its models.

These automated processes do not by themselves make final decisions about your account status. Flagged content is handled per our Terms.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

4.1 Other Users

  • Your public profile (name, username, photo, vibes, prompts) is visible to other users
  • Events you attend are visible to other attendees (unless you disable "Show Attending Events" in Privacy Settings)
  • Your messages in event group chats are visible to other group members

4.2 Service Providers (Sub-processors)

  • Google Firebase (Google Cloud Platform, USA): authentication, database (Firestore), file storage, cloud functions, push notifications (FCM), analytics, crash reporting (Crashlytics), app integrity (App Check)
  • Cloudflare R2 (Global CDN): event data caching for faster loading
  • SightEngine (EU): image content moderation
  • Google Gemini AI (USA): text content moderation, optional description generation, and face verification
  • Vercel (USA / global): hosting of the dayzi.live website
  • EmailOctopus: managing waitlist / early-registration emails submitted on the Site

These providers process data on our behalf under their respective privacy policies and data-processing agreements.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your data if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Dayzi, our users, or the public.

5. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) servers located in the United States and may be processed by our sub-processors in the USA, EU, and elsewhere. By using Dayzi, you consent to the transfer of your data to these countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place as required by applicable data-protection laws.

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: we retain your data while your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: after you request account deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days. During this grace period you can restore your account by logging back in.
  • Chat messages: retained while the event group is active; archived locally on your device when you leave a group.
  • Analytics data: aggregated, anonymised analytics data may be retained indefinitely.
  • Push notification tokens: deleted from our servers when you log out or delete your account.

7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law (including India's DPDP Act 2023, the GDPR, and the CCPA/CPRA), you have the following rights:

7.1 Access and Portability

  • View your data within the App (profile, events, messages)
  • Export a copy of your personal data in JSON format from Privacy Settings → Download My Data

7.2 Correction

  • Update your profile information at any time from Edit Profile

7.3 Deletion (Right to Erasure)

  • Delete your account and all associated data from Privacy Settings → Delete Account. See our Delete Account page for full details.
  • Account deletion includes: profile data, profile photo, events created, messages, notifications, group memberships, FCM tokens, and all cached data

7.4 Consent Withdrawal

  • You may withdraw consent by deleting your account
  • You may disable push notifications from your device settings or by logging out
  • You may hide your attending events from other users via Privacy Settings → Show Attending Events

7.5 Restriction and Objection

  • Contact us at dayzibusiness@gmail.com to request restriction of processing or to object to specific data uses

California residents (CCPA/CPRA): you have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request its deletion, to correct it, and not to be discriminated against for exercising your rights. We do not sell personal information.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Firebase App Check to verify app integrity
  • Firebase Authentication for secure account access
  • Access controls restricting data access to authorised services only
  • Automated content moderation to prevent abuse
  • Rate limiting on sensitive operations

No system is 100% secure. Please keep your login credentials confidential.

9. Children's Privacy

Dayzi is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will promptly delete their account and data. If you believe a minor is using Dayzi, please contact us at dayzibusiness@gmail.com.

10. Push Notifications

We send push notifications for event-related updates (invitations, approvals, cancellations, etc.) and occasional admin announcements. You can:

  • Disable push notifications from your device's system settings
  • Unsubscribe from broadcast notifications by logging out (topic subscription is cleared on logout)

FCM tokens are stored in your user profile and deleted when you log out or delete your account.

11. Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use browser cookies. We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data in the App. No third-party advertising trackers or ad SDKs are used. The dayzi.live website may use cookies strictly necessary for the site to function and to process waitlist sign-ups.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via in-app notification, push notification, or on the Site. Continued use of Dayzi after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
